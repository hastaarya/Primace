Accounting and Bookkeeping

Tax Services

Frequently Asked Questions

We know that financial and tax matters can be complex. This section answers the most common questions about our accounting, tax, and advisory services — helping you understand how Prima Akuntansi Cemerlang can support your business with clarity, accuracy, and trust.

1. What services do you offer?

We specialize in accounting, bookkeeping, tax consulting, financial reporting, payroll, and business advisory services for individuals and businesses.

2. Who are your services for?

Our services are suitable for small to medium businesses (UKM/UMKM), startups, individual entrepreneurs, and corporations who need reliable financial and tax management.

3. Can you handle monthly bookkeeping?

Yes, we provide regular (monthly or quarterly) bookkeeping services to keep your financial records accurate and up to date.

4. Do you offer tax reporting services?

Absolutely. We assist with monthly and annual tax reporting (SPT), PPh 21/23/25, PPN, and tax planning strategies to ensure compliance and efficiency.

5. Can you help with company setup or PT establishment?

Yes, we can guide and assist you in setting up a new business, including PT or CV formation, and NPWP registration.

6. Are your services confidential and secure?

Yes, confidentiality is our top priority. We follow strict standards to ensure your financial data is safe and secure.

7. How much do your services cost?

Our pricing depends on the complexity and scope of the work. Contact us for a free consultation and customized quote.

8. How do I get started?

Simply contact us via our website, WhatsApp, or email. We’ll schedule a free consultation to understand your needs and recommend the right solution.
